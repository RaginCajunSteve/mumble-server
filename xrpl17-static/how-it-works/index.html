How it works

Built for retail traders who want to follow proven on-chain performers without giving up custody. We support Pro mirrors on EVM chains + free instant client-side mirrors on Solana and BSC.

Production site · Live execution on Base mainnet, Arbitrum One, Optimism

  • Leaderboard index: Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon, Unichain, Linea, Blast, Optimism, BSC, Avalanche, Scroll mainnet
  • Keeper watch (alpha activity): Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism mainnet
  • Live execution networks: Base mainnet, Arbitrum One, Optimism
    • Base mainnet: 0x1e282104…
    • Arbitrum One: 0xefe995d1…
    • Optimism: 0xefe995d1…
  • Paper mirrors simulate PnL when activity is detected — no funds move.
  • Live mirrors submit on-chain txs on Base mainnet, Arbitrum One, Optimism when your mirror includes those chains and allowances are set.
  1. 1

    We index DEX swaps

    Across Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism we track Uniswap-style swaps and reconstruct closed positions.

  2. 2

    We score risk-adjusted ROI

    ROI divided by max drawdown. Rolling 30d, 90d, and 180d windows. A daily indexer rescans DEX activity; top 0.5% make the leaderboard.

  3. 3

    We decode the playbook

    Hold time, chain preference, entry patterns — written for retail users, not quants.

  4. 4

    You mirror with caps

    Connect MetaMask, set per-trade and daily limits, choose paper or live mode. Funds stay in your wallet.

  5. 5

    Bot executes on alpha moves (Pro EVM)

    When the elite wallet moves on Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, our Cloudflare keeper cron detects it, scales to your caps, and either simulates (paper) or submits on-chain txs (live on Base mainnet, Arbitrum One, Optimism).

  6. 6

    Free instant mirrors (Solana + BSC)

    Separate zero-cost path: tracked alphas trigger real-time pendings via Helius (Solana) or free RPCs (BSC). You sign the Jupiter or Pancake tx client-side directly from the dashboard. No server gas, no subscription required for this path.

  7. 7

    You stay in control

    Pause anytime. Revoke allowances to stop completely. Send feedback on any page to help us improve.

Bonus: Free instant mirrors (Solana + BSC)

In addition to configured Pro mirrors, we run a separate zero-cost tracker. When tracked Solana elites trade (Helius webhooks), or BSC elites trade on Pancake, pendings surface on your dashboard. You sign a direct Jupiter or Pancake swap in your wallet. No fees to us, no subscriptions required for this path. See the guide on the dashboard or live mirror setup for details.
